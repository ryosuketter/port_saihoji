Admission
Hours		 Generally, we would like for you to follow the one-entrance-per-day rule.
We are open at 10:00 a.m. during July to September, and at 01:00 p.m.
for other months.
  • Please arrive about 15 minutes before your reserved time.
  • There may be changes with the hours without notice. And it is not possible to specify a visit time.
  • Reservations only. Please checkthe reservation sectionfor details.
Fee 3,000 JPY per person (Cash only)
Contents Visit to the garden, after the religious rituals at the Hondō.
Visit duration Depends on each visitor, but most take 60 ~ 90 minutes.
Seats There are a few seats available at the Hondō, no pre-reservations.
Although it is common to sit in “seiza”, it is not required.
